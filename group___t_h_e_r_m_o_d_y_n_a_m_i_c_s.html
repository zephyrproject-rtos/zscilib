Calculates the energy of the hot chamber of a Carnot engine when the energy of the cold chamber and the temperatures in both chambers are known values.

Parameters
tcTemperature in the cold chamber in kelvins.
qcEnergy of the cold chamber in joules.
thTemperature in the hot chamber in kelvins.
qhPointer to the output energy of the hot chamber in joules. Will be set to NAN if any of the temperatures is negative or if tc = 0.
Returns
0 if everything executed properly, error code on failure.
