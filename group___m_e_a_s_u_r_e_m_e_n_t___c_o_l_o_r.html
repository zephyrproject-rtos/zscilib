enum zsl_mes_ext_color

Extended measuremnt types for ZSL_MES_TYPE_COLOR (8-bit).

Memory map:

  • 0 = RGB+A 8-bit integers (32-bit color)
  • 0x10..0x1F = RGB+A triplets
  • 0x30..0x4F = CIE values
  • 0x50..0xFE = Reserved
Enumerator
